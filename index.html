SQC — Quantum Computing Club

Welcome to SQC, the Quantum Computing Club at NIT Jalandhar! We are a collective of forward-thinking students united by our shared passion for Quantum Computing, IoT, and Cryptography. Our club fosters curiosity, hands-on learning, and collaborative innovation.

Who We Are

A student-led community exploring quantum algorithms, secure communications, and embedded systems. We welcome learners at all levels.

Our Vision

Exploration • Inclusivity • Innovation — we aim to create an inclusive environment where novel ideas can take root and students can gain cutting-edge skills.

Why Join

Workshops, hands-on projects, and community collaboration that keep members at the forefront of emerging tech. Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Jalandhar, Punjab.
